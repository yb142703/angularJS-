limitTo(向前限制)：-->{{ [1,2,3,4,5,6,7] | limitTo:5 }}

limitTo(向后限制)：-->{{ [1,2,3,4,5,6,7] | limitTo:-5 }}

lowercase(转小写字母):--> {{ data.message | lowercase}}

uppercase(转大写字母):--> {{ data.message | uppercase}}

filter(过滤对象内容 匹配Value值):-->{{ data.city | filter:"山西" }}

filter(过滤对象内容:{**:'**'}):-->{{ data.city | filter: {py:'g'} }}

添加拓展过滤:
filter(过滤对象内容:{**:'**'}):-->{{ data.city | filter: checkName }}

orderBy(排序):-->{{ data.city | orderBy:'py' }}
**默认顺序:-> 键的值(py:???)是正序 A~Z

orderBy(排序):-->{{ data.city | orderBy:'-py' }}
**加-顺序:-> 键的值(py:???)是逆序 Z~A
